Space Complexity of Merge Sort
---------------------------------------------------
The space complexity of Merge Sort is linear space.
This means that the amount of extra memory the algorithm requires grows proportionally with the size of the input array

Quick Sort - Efficient 
--------------------------------------
Quick Sort has a space complexity of $O(\log n)$ on average (and $O(n)$ in the worst-case scenario). 
This makes Quick Sort significantly more space-efficient than Merge Sort
